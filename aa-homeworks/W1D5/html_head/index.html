Salmon

Description

Salmon is a kind of fish that you cook on a hot surface and then eat it.

Ingredients

  • A piece of salmon
  • Some olive oil
  • A pan
  • Some butter, probably?
  • A full lemon

Directions

  1. Heat up a pan to medium-heat.
  2. Cook the salmon on the pan.
  3. Use the other ingredients at some point.
  4. Eat it.
